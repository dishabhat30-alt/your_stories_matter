🔒 Your Privacy & Safety First

Your story will be completely anonymous. We are committed to protecting your identity and creating a safe space for you to share.

Here's our promise to you:

  • Complete anonymity: We will never share your email or personal information
  • Identity protection: All identifying details will be carefully removed from your story
  • Your consent matters: We will only use your story with your explicit permission
  • Safe sharing: Stories are shared as animated content to further protect your identity

💜 Why Your Story Matters

Every story shared here helps break the silence and shows others they're not alone. Your courage in sharing can be a beacon of hope for someone else walking a similar path.

Whether you're in the midst of your journey or years into healing, your voice deserves to be heard. Your experience, your strength, and your survival matter.
